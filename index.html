Comprehensive Features

Legal Prejudice Framework

Structured methodology based on 28 U.S.C. §§ 455, 144 and key Supreme Court precedents including Liteky v. United States and Caperton v. A.T. Massey Coal Co.

Risk & Probability Analysis

Quantitative methods including likelihood ratio analysis, Monte Carlo simulations, and Bayesian probability for evidence-based assessment.

Practical Implementation

48-hour prejudice triage assessment protocol, strategic response options, and documentation templates for tracking potential prejudice.

Interactive Risk Calculator

Web-based calculator for assessing prejudice factors with visualization of risk through matrix display and recommendation generation.

API & Integration

Comprehensive API with endpoints for assessments, factors, results, and webhooks for real-time notifications.

Case Studies

Detailed analysis of example scenarios demonstrating practical application of the framework in various prejudice situations.
